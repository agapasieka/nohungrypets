✦ How it works

Simple as a bowl of kibble

Three easy steps to share food with pets in your area — no fees, no fuss, no forms to fill in.

01
📝

Sign up in under a minute. Tell us your location and what animals you have — we'll show you the most relevant listings nearby.

02
📦

Post what you have to give

Got leftover kibble, unopened tins or unused supplies? Post a listing in seconds — add a photo, a description and your location.
