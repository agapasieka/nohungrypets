Community Guidelines

Welcome to NoHungryPets! Our mission is to ensure no pet goes hungry by connecting animal lovers who have surplus food and supplies with those who need them. To keep our community safe, friendly, and helpful, please follow these guidelines:

1. Safety First

  • Quality of items: Please only share pet food that is unexpired, unspoiled, and safe for consumption. Opened bags of dry kibble are generally fine if properly stored, but avoid sharing opened wet food.
  • Accurate descriptions: Be honest about the condition of the items you are sharing. Include clear photos and mention if the food is tailored to specific dietary needs or allergies.
  • Safe pickups: When arranging to hand over items, choose safe, public, well-lit locations, or use contactless porch pickups if you feel comfortable doing so.

2. Free and Fair

  • Everything is free: NoHungryPets is a platform for sharing, not selling. You may not ask for money, trades, or compensation for any items listed on this site.
  • Take only what you need: We operate on an honor system. Please be considerate of others in the community and only claim items that your pets genuinely need.

3. Respect and Kindness

  • Be courteous: Treat all members of the NoHungryPets community with kindness and respect. Harassment, hate speech, or abuse will not be tolerated and will result in an immediate ban.
  • Communicate clearly: If you claim an item, communicate promptly with the poster to arrange pickup. If you can no longer pick it up, let them know so they can offer it to someone else.

4. Prohibited Items

  • Medications (prescription or non-prescription)
  • Live animals (NoHungryPets is NOT a rehoming platform)
  • Expired or visibly spoiled food
  • Recalled pet food items

By using NoHungryPets, you agree to abide by these guidelines. We reserve the right to remove listings or suspend accounts that violate these rules. Thank you for helping us feed pets in need!
