✦ About

Helping pets eat, one bowl at a time

NoHungryPets is a simple community idea: share surplus pet food and supplies locally — completely free.

What we’re building

Sometimes people have unopened tins, extra kibble, or supplies their pets no longer need. At the same time, someone nearby might be struggling to cover the next bag of food.

NoHungryPets aims to make it easy to match those two people — quickly, safely, and locally.

  • Free to use
  • All animals welcome
  • Local-first (neighbourhood sharing)
